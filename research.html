Introduction of the research paper

Physics, as my discipline which will go along with me in these four college years will be the topic of the research paper. This research paper will not only help readers but also help myself to get a better vision and a better understanding about this important science discipline, Physics. This research paper can be composed by five major parts. For the first part, I am going introduce my discipline. For the second part, I am going to talk about communications in the discipline. For the third part, I am going to analysis the relationship between technology and the discipline. For the fourth part, I am going to talk about way of leaning in Physics class and research in physics. For the fifth part, I will summarize and display the interview information that I recorded from the people who are related to the discipline.
