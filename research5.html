Interview

Interview with Professor Huss.

Mr. Huss is my physics teacher for my junior year in Phoenix Christian High school. He is a professor on both chemistry and physics. By calling him on the phone for a phone interview, I summarize some questions and answers.

Why do you like physics, this discipline?

God creates the world. Physics helps me to know this world better. So I can know God better.

What can I do after I graduate as a physics major student?

It’s better for you to continue studying for PhD degree then get a job. It will be fine if you want to find a job without PhD degree, physics has a wide career range. Anything which is related to physics would give you an opportunity for a job. You can anticipate in building and structures, energy, transports, education all kind of fields. But it will easier to find a job with PhD degree.

What Skills Do I Need to Study Physics? How can I improve my Physics?

Mathematics will be a tool for Physics. With good mathematics knowledge will help you to get better understanding in physics.

All science are related. Study other fields of science, such as biology and chemistry will get you a different view of physics and understanding of it.

Interview with an elder classmate who study physics, Ray Zhang.

Why do you choose physics as your major?

I can still be a business man without learning business in college. But science major like physics will give you real knowledge which will go along with you for the whole life.

Do you think physics is hard to learn?

It depends on person. If you work hard and you have a good logical way of thinking. I don’t think it will be hard for you.

How to learn physics better?

You should take a good notes from the class for reviewing. Don’t ever copy homework from other. Physics needs your own way of thinking to solve the questions. You should do a lot of practice. Practice will help you to remember the way of solving questions.
